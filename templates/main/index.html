What is Dance Contraption?

Dance Contraption is:

  • Brand new, and pretty useless right now. Find Brandon Martin-Anderson (badhill@gmail.com) and tell him what you think!
  • A tool for effective dance gypsying.
  • A place to host a community history of how callers, bands, events, and dancers interacted and crossed paths, and how contra has spread and changed over time.
  • A place to moving dance organization off of Facebook and onto a place that doesn't exist explicitly to exploit community interactions for commercial gain.

Also!

  • Dance Contraption is open source. Dance Contraption github page.
  • Use github to file bugs and feature requests.
  • Sam Kleinman made a contra wiki at http://contra.balanceandsing.com/.
  • Don't pronounce it "dance contraception" in your head.
